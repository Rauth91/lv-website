Award-Winning Craftsmanship

Our Bourbon Collection

Straight Bourbon

Woody, warm sippin’ whiskey. Good for business, tailgating, and duck huntin’. It can be offloaded to flasks and plastic cups without bruising. Give some to your grandfather and get a lecture on generational tastes. It all has a good clean finish without the burn of other whiskeys.

Reserve Bourbon

Aged for 2 years in Midwest white oak barrels, Laissez Versez Reserve Whiskey resonates honied tones that excite the palate just as your favorite music pleases your ear. Luxuriant golden, and smooth, like drinking the summer twilight.

Cask Bourbon

Cask strength, Laissez Versez whiskey sips easy and blends better. Chill with a Julep on a hot afternoon, share an Old-Fashioned with friends, or relax at the end of a long day with a Hot Toddy. This is a coy whiskey, offering up subtle undertones of oak, vanilla, and spice.

Devious Bourbon

After we empty a barrel and cap it closed, the bourbon residing in the wood itself weeps back into the barrel. Deviously hidden… and now bottled and available for your enjoyment.

Our Legacy

Laissez Versez aims to revive Bourbon-making in Louisiana by creating a high-quality local craft Bourbon with rich history and unique flavors. We prioritize using local ingredients and vendors, and take pride in being a part of the distilling community.

Our goal from day one was to create a quality Louisiana Bourbon that's easy on the palate. We do this by pushing the barrels past the traditional standard aging process to capture a Bourbon that's pleasant on the nose with strong notes of oak and vanilla- easy on the palate, with hints of caramel, and a long savory finish.

Let the good times pour with Laissez Versez Bourbon!
